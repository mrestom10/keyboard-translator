Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Translator Keyboard ("the App") is a keyboard application that provides real-time translation capabilities. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Translator Keyboard is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function:

  • Text for Translation: When you tap the Translate button, the text you typed is sent to third-party translation services (MyMemory, Google Translate) to perform the translation. This text is sent directly to these services and is NOT stored by us.
  • GIF/Sticker Searches: When you search for GIFs or stickers, your search query is sent to GIPHY's API. We do not store these queries.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT log, store, or transmit your keystrokes
  • We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers
  • We do NOT store your translation history
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or files

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services for its core functionality:

  • MyMemory Translation API (mymemory.translated.net) - for text translation. Their privacy policy applies to data sent to their servers.
  • Google Translate (translate.googleapis.com) - as a fallback translation service. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • GIPHY (api.giphy.com) - for GIF and sticker search. GIPHY's privacy policy applies to search queries.

4. Data Storage

All keyboard processing happens locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers by us. Translation text is sent to third-party APIs only when you explicitly tap the Translate button, and is not retained after the translation is complete.

5. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required to connect to translation APIs and GIPHY for GIF search.
  • BIND_INPUT_METHOD: Required by Android for the app to function as a keyboard (Input Method Editor).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Security

All network communication uses HTTPS encryption. The App does not store sensitive data locally or remotely.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated "Effective Date" above.
